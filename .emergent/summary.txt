<original_problem_statement>
The user wants to build a mobile and web application for booking DJs for events.

**Product Requirements:**
-   **User Roles**: DJs, Event Organizers, and an Admin.
-   **DJ Profiles**: Include details like music style, experience, performance videos, and social media links (including TikTok).
-   **Event & DJ Discovery**: Search and filter for DJs and events.
-   **Booking System**: A complete flow for organizers to book DJs, including a dedicated checkout page.
-   **Payment System**:
    -   Organizers pay for bookings via Stripe.
    -   The platform takes a 15% commission on each transaction.
    -   DJ earnings are held in a pending balance for 24 hours after the organizer confirms the service is complete.
    -   After 24 hours, funds become available.
    -   DJs can request a withdrawal of their available balance (minimum 50€) to their IBAN or PayPal.
-   **Messaging**: A feature for DJs and Organizers to communicate.
-   **UI/UX**:
    -   A Neon Style theme with a dark background and neon colors.
    -   Custom popups for errors and confirmations.
    -   A responsive web version with a classic desktop layout (top navbar, centered content).
-   **Admin Dashboard**: An interface for the admin to view transactions and commissions.
</original_problem_statement>
